ROMEO AND/OR JULIET
AND/OR GRAPH THEORY

It is not in the stars to hold our destiny but in ourselves.
-William Shakespeare

The below constellation of choices represents all possible paths and endings present in Ryan North's wonderful chooseable path adventure Romeo and/or Juliet. There are some spoilers here, but I hope this encourages you to pick up a copy of the book! Its truely creative, hillarious and even insightful about the plot for Shakespeare's Romeo and Juliet. Gave me a new appreciation. One of the pleasures of Ryan's interpretation is that of discovery and exploration, seeing the gorgous art and potential other paths tease you as you flip between passages for choices you've made and I hope that this project allows a different kind of exploration and discovery of the work, showing the larger structure with branching and converging fates of the characters as you make your choices through the story.

This page was created using D3.js's force directed layout, SVG and good old fashioned data entry gumption. Dots represent the story passages and arrows the choices between them. You can hover over a passage or choice to see more information about it. The color of the passage represents the POV character. Choices that direct you to follow the path of Shakespeare's version of the story are red, while Ryan North's elaborations are white. Larger dots are endings, while the largest dots are the story's beginning and ending. The layout is generated automatically and will be different every time. If it gets a little tangled, you can click and drag passages around to get a better look. **You can also zoom using your mouse's scroll wheel.**
